# Examples

## Legend

{S … }  subject frame (solid white frame; used only when a subject role is established)  
{V … }  verb frame (flowing white frame; predicate head)  
{O … }  object frame (pulsating white frame; direct object head)

( … )   lexical element or modifier (unframed)  
|       pause / darkness (group boundary; establishes clause structure)

---

## 1. Greeting

**toki pona:**  
`toki!`

*hello*

**structure:**  
`(toki)`

**colour rendering:**  
`(Purple)`

---

## 2. Noun phrase (bird without using waso)

**toki pona:**  
`soweli kon`

*bird (lit. air animal)*

**structure:**  
`(soweli) (kon)`  

**colour rendering:**  
`(Light Blue + Brown + Red) (Dark Blue)`

---

## 3. Noun phrase with colour

**toki pona:**  
`soweli kon kule loje`

*red bird*

**structure:**  
`(soweli) (kon) (kule loje)`  

**colour rendering:**  
`(Light Blue + Brown + Red) (Dark Blue) (Brown + Light Blue + Dark Blue) (Red)`

---

## 4. Intransitive predicate

**toki pona:**  
`soweli li moku`

*the animal eats*

**structure:**  
`{S soweli} | {V moku}`  

**colour rendering:**  
`{S Light Blue + Brown + Red} | {V Dark Blue + Red}`

---

## 5. First person predicates with modifiers

**toki pona:**  
`mi moku pona`  

*I eat well / I eat good food*

**structure:**  
`{S mi} | {V moku} (pona)`

---

**toki pona:**  
`mi pona moku`  

*I am good at eating*

**structure:**  
`{S mi} | {V pona} (moku)`

---

## 6. Transitive predicate

**toki pona:**  
`jan li moku e kili`

*the person eats fruit*

**structure:**  
`{S jan} | {V moku} | {O kili}`

---

## 7. Predicate adjective

**toki pona:**  
`jan li pona`

*the person is good*

**structure:**  
`{S jan} | {V pona}`

---

## 8. Context setting

**toki pona:**  
`tenpo lape la jan li moku`

*when sleeping / after sleeping, the person eats*

**structure:**  
`(tenpo) (lape) (la) | {S jan} | {V moku}`

---

## 9. Negation

**toki pona:**  
`jan li moku ala`

*the person does not eat*

**structure:**  
`{S jan} | {V moku} (ala)`

---

## 10. Question

**toki pona:**  
`jan seme li moku`

*who eats?*

**structure:**  
`{S jan} (seme) | {V moku}`

**colour rendering:**  
`{S Red + Light Blue + Yellow} (flashing white frame) | {V Dark Blue + Red}`
